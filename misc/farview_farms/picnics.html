WHY HAVE A COMPANY PICNIC?

There are lots of reasons to have a company picnic at Farview Farms. Sure, it’s a great excuse to get out of the office; but look beyond the obvious and see why a company picnic can be critical to your organization. For one thing, it’s BEING PART OF A TEAM. This is an opportunity to show your employees how much you value their contribution. It lets them know they’re an important part of a whole team effort. And that means increased camaraderie and renewed trust, which can lead to increased productivity. AN OLD-FASHIONED PICNIC IS RIGHT IN STYLE: There is simply no substitute for an old-fashioned picnic at Farview Farms. Together, employees can enjoy the beautiful tall woods, dramatic ravine, winding Silver Creek, and long country vistas. They can revel in the variety of activities available. Here, there are no rides to run away to or take attention away from the real purpose of the event – to be together as a company, talking one-on-one with all levels of employees, meeting on the common ground of a volleyball court or a creek side trail. We look forward to enjoying many years of sharing a very special 50 acres of Michigan’s natural beauty with you, our honored guest.
